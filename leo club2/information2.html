This club includes meetings every Wednesday at 3:40 pm. in room 111. Mrs. Hansen usually presents different opportunities for the students to volunteer and have them sign up. She also gives them a varity of other opportunities for volunteer experiences that the students can do on their own time; which doesn't include the whole club.

Some examples include volunteering at Festival of Trees, help set up Homecoming. The club has help at A Night to Shine which was a special needs ball by the Tim Tebow Foundation. The club has also helped with purchases from Angel Tree, campus clean-ups and many bake-sales.

Leo Club helps teaches students how to be respectful, caring and thoughtful to others. It's an amazing learning experience how help can go a long way and change peoples lives for the better. Not only are you doing good for others, you also better yourself in the process.

This club can be an eye opener to most and is beneficial for the students in their future. Also another benefit from joining this club it looks good on a college résumé.
